About Us

Over the years, failure rate in English Language and General Mathematics has been comparatively higher than it is with other school subjects, the persistent high failure rates have been attributed to many factors including low quality of teachers and deplorable state of teaching and learning facilities in schools. The few trained teachers are themselves products of lowly pedagogical standard. Motivation among teaching staff members is low especially in public schools. Most of those that partake in the January series of WASSCE are private candidates who failed to obtain minimum credit passes in relevant subjects in previous attempts. Unfortunately, these groups of students are poorly prepared for the examination.

Edu Home is connecting highly motivated and standard tutors in mathematics and English Language with Exam year students across West Africa who do not have access to quality and expensive education virtually for Free.Edu home also offer virtual live support to students who find it difficulty in researching for quality contents from the largest and most voluminous library in the world (the internet) Also our virtual support, listen to the complains of students and recommend credible guide for them to arrive at their desired information resolve using machine learning, we are simply improving how students search for educational resources online too with our Ask us function.
